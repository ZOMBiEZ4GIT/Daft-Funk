## Usage Guide

### Energy Level Organization

Each genre is organized into 5 energy-level batches of 20 sets each:

1. **Opening (x01-x20):** 4-6/10 energy - Warm opener sets for early night
2. **Warmup (x21-x40):** 6-7/10 energy - Building momentum and groove
3. **Peak Time (x41-x60):** 8-9/10 energy - Maximum dancefloor energy
4. **Late Night (x61-x80):** 5-7/10 energy - Deep, hypnotic vibes
5. **After Hours (x81-x00):** 3-5/10 energy - Comedown and chill sessions

### Set Format

Each 60-minute set includes:
- BPM range and energy level
- Genre and style descriptors
- Artist/track information (varies by set)
- Mixing instructions (detailed sets only)
- Harmonic key information (detailed sets only)
